Meet the Creator

Behind Lúmina Nabi is a dreamer with a deep love for storytelling through design. I created this brand from the belief that wedding invitations should be more than paper—they should be keepsakes that glow with meaning, reflect a couple’s unique journey, and set the tone for a day filled with love.

With an eye for delicate details and a heart for celebrating love stories, I pour passion into every piece—whether it’s a ready-made collection or a fully customized design. For me, it’s not just about stationery, but about creating something couples can hold onto forever, a little reminder of the light and beauty that began it all.

Through Lúmina Nabi, I hope to bring your vision to life and craft invitations that feel just as unforgettable as the love they represent.
